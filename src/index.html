Our goal at SkillSync is to turn the tech hiring process on its head and create a system that works better for both employers and candidates alike.

We want to help employers find better candidates before they reach the interview stage and to help candidates attract employers who need the skills they have.

By focusing on skills, we hope to encourage employers and candidates to think beyond the job description and move into a process that is simpler and produces better results.

We want to simplify the tech recruiting process for both employers and candidates by focusing on what matters most.

Anonymity

Employers pitch jobs to you based on your listed skills. You decide whether or not to move forward.

Skills Based

Match based on the essential skills list for a specific job, rather than a laundry list of nice to haves.

Resume Building

We'll help you build the perfect resume to land you the dream job you've always wanted.

Candidate Search

We'll help you find the perfect candidate. Our algorithms will sift through the talent pool for you, alerting you to those with the highest number of matching essential skills. We can even send you an email when new matching candidates sign up.

Reverse Job Board

Employers will apply to you, with a detailed job description, company info, and salary range.
